Policies &
Safeguarding

We operate with the highest standards of integrity, transparency, and child protection. These policies guide everything we do.

Child Safeguarding is Our Priority

ACSS has zero tolerance for any form of child abuse, exploitation, or neglect. All staff, volunteers, and partners must adhere to our safeguarding policy.

24/7 Safeguarding Hotline: +211 912 345 679
