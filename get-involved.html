Partner With Us

ACSS offers meaningful partnership opportunities for corporations, foundations, and institutions that want to demonstrate genuine social impact in one of the world's most challenging humanitarian contexts.

Transparent Impact Reporting

Receive quarterly impact reports detailing exactly how your partnership funds are deployed and the outcomes achieved.

Co-Branded Campaigns

Joint communications, social media content, and cause-marketing campaigns that tell your company's commitment to child rights.

Staff Volunteer Programs

Skilled volunteering placements for your employees — from legal and finance to communications and technology.

Recognition & Visibility

Logo placement on ACSS materials, website partner page, and annual report. Recognition at key events and on social media.
