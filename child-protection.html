Child Protection

Every child has the right to live free from violence, exploitation, and abuse. Our child protection program is the frontline response to that right.

Protecting Children at Risk

In South Sudan's conflict zones, children face separation from families, abduction by armed groups, gender-based violence, and statelessness. Our child protection teams work 24/7 to identify, rescue, and provide immediate care to children at risk.

What We Do

  • Identify and rescue children from abusive situations
  • Provide emergency shelter and care in safe houses
  • Offer legal aid and birth registration services
  • Support family tracing and reunification
  • Train communities on child protection and awareness

Services

Our Child Protection Services

Comprehensive protection for every child in need.

SAFE

Safe Houses & Emergency Shelters

Secure, staffed emergency shelters providing immediate refuge for children in crisis, operating across 8 locations in South Sudan.

LEGAL

Legal Aid & Documentation

Birth registration, legal identity documentation, and advocacy for children's rights within South Sudan's legal system.

RAPID

Emergency Response Teams

Rapid response units deployed within 24 hours of a reported child protection incident, including mobile case management teams.

CASE

Case Management

Individual case plans for every child, with dedicated social workers ensuring continuity of care from crisis to recovery.

FAMILY

Family Tracing & Reunification

Locating and reuniting children with their families, including cross-border tracing and community mediation.

AWARE

Community Awareness

Training communities to recognize and report child protection concerns, creating safer environments for children.
